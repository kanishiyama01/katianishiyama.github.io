## Welcome to my website!

I am a Professor of Economics at Mackenzie University and FGV.

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Fundação Getulio Vargas – EPGE. I also earned a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from FGV EPGE and a Licentiate degree in Mathematics from Universidade Estadual Paulista (UNESP).

My research combines theoretical and applied approaches, with particular interest in gender-related policies, matching markets, and education.
